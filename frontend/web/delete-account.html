Delete Your Account

ZERA Business  ·  Account & Data Deletion Request

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your store data, products, orders, and customer records will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your business profile and store information
  • All products and inventory data
  • All orders and transaction history
  • Customer records linked to your store
  • Your account credentials and login data

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: From the App

  1. Open the ZERA Business app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to support@zeramai.com
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
Request Deletion via Email

Data Retention

After deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. We may retain minimal transaction records as required by Indian financial regulations (GST compliance) for up to 7 years, but these records will not be linked to your personal identity.
